# Add your logo

## How to Add Your Logo

1. Go to your Plox **dashboard**
2. Click on **“Branding”** from the left-side panel
3. Under the **“Logo”** section, click **Upload Logo**
4. Choose a high-quality image (PNG or JPG recommended)
5. Hit **Save** — you're done!

Your logo will now appear across all future document and data room views.

## Where Viewers Will See Your Logo

Once added, your logo will appear:

- In the **document viewer header** (top bar)
- Inside **data room previews**
- On **mobile and desktop** viewing experiences
- Alongside your **company name**, if set

This gives viewers immediate confidence that the file came from **your brand**, not a random link.

- Builds **trust and authenticity**

## FAQs

- Where can I upload my logo in Plox?

  Go to the Branding section from the left-hand menu in your dashboard. Under the “Logo” section, click Upload Logo, select your image, and save.
- What file types are supported for logos?

  You can upload PNG, JPG, or JPEG files. PNG with a transparent background is recommended for best results.
- How big should my logo be?

  A minimum of 300x300 pixels works well. Keep file sizes under 2 MB for smooth performance.
- Where will my logo appear for viewers?

  Your logo will appear at the top of the document viewer and data room interface, along with your company name (if set).
- Can I change or update my logo later?

  Yes. You can upload a new logo anytime from the Branding section—your changes will apply instantly to new views.
- Will my logo appear on all shared documents?

  Yes, once added, your logo appears on all shared links and data rooms, unless you're on the Free plan.
- Can I remove the Plox logo and only show mine?

  Yes, but only on the Pro plan, which supports full white-labeling (no Plox branding at all).
